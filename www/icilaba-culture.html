Icilaba Culture is a recent research and development structure. We aim to be a creative answer to industry, as much as breaking into new markets as developing existing ones. Launched in France, Icilaba Culture has an identity linked with the future, being both without borders and respecting individual cultures.
Icilaba Culture is a working, fresh thinking and creative partner for business. Its structure develops a blog dedicated to various cultures, a multimedia library, a material database and numerous newsletters.
